# Role Requirements: AI Infrastructure Architect

**Level:** 3
**Experience Level:** 8-12 years in ML/data engineering, systems architecture, or related fields, with at least 3-5 years in architecture roles
**Market Demand:** Critical Shortage - Organizations struggling to find qualified AI Infrastructure Architects with enterprise-scale experience. High demand from Fortune 500 companies, major tech firms, and enterprises undergoing digital transformation.
**Salary Range (US, 2025):** $165,000 - $350,000 (median $210,000)

## Core Responsibilities
- Design end-to-end architecture for enterprise-scale AI/ML platforms
- Define technical strategy and roadmap for AI infrastructure
- Architect multi-cloud and hybrid AI deployment solutions
- Design high-availability, fault-tolerant ML systems for mission-critical applications
- Establish architectural patterns and standards for ML infrastructure
- Lead evaluation and selection of AI/ML technologies and platforms
- Design cost-optimization strategies for large-scale AI workloads
- Create security and compliance architecture for AI systems
- Collaborate with business leaders to translate requirements into technical architecture
- Design scalable feature stores and real-time data platforms
- Architect model lifecycle management and governance frameworks
- Lead cross-functional architecture review boards
- Design disaster recovery and business continuity strategies for AI systems
- Mentor architects and senior engineers across the organization
- Drive architectural innovation and research initiatives
