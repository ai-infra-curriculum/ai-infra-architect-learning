# Interview Readiness Guide – AI Infrastructure Architect

## Purpose
Adapt the architect repository into interview prep focused on enterprise design, governance, and stakeholder alignment.

## Core Competencies to Highlight
- Enterprise architecture frameworks and reference designs  
- Multi-cloud/hybrid strategy and cost models  
- Governance artefacts (RACI, escalation maps, decision logs)  
- Executive communication and cross-functional influence

## Preparation Checklist
1. Pick two architecture blueprints and one governance artefact that demonstrate your impact.  
2. Link each to a business outcome (cost reduction, resilience, time-to-market).  
3. Prepare visual aids (C4 diagrams, contract checklists) for screen-share discussions.  
4. Rehearse explanations of trade-offs and stakeholder negotiations.

## Mock Interview Structure (60 minutes)
| Segment | Objective | Repository References |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 10 min | Background + architectural philosophy | Root README |
| 20 min | Deep dive on a reference architecture | `projects/project-302` etc. |
| 15 min | Governance and process alignment | Governance module |
| 10 min | Negotiation or executive communication scenario | Decision logs |
| 5 min | Candidate questions | Prepared list |

## Sample Technical Prompts
- “Describe how you’d design a multi-region AI platform with compliance constraints.”  
- “How do you evaluate build vs buy for core infrastructure components?”  
- “Walk through the cost model and governance gates for your most recent architecture.”

## Behavioral Prompts
- Aligning conflicting executive priorities  
- Handling escalations when architecture standards are challenged  
- Coaching teams through major migrations

## Questions to Ask the Interviewer
- “How does your company fund and prioritise architecture initiatives?”  
- “What artefacts are most valued during executive reviews?”  
- “How do you measure success for infrastructure architects?”

## Next Steps
Create a personal dossier: include links to blueprints, decision logs, and stakeholder maps. Iterate after each interview to refine talking points.***
